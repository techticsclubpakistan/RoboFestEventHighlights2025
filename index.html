ROS2 Humble Workshop

Join the exciting "Robots in Action with ROS2" workshop, part of RoboFest 2025, organized by TechTics Club to take your robotics knowledge beyond just coding!

📍 Venue: Beaconhouse PECHS Campus
📅 Dates: 27–28 September
🎯 Format: Live Session

What You'll Learn:

  • ROS 2 Humble – The latest version of the Robot Operating System
  • Edge AI – Learn how AI works on the edge
  • Embedded Systems – Merge software with hardware
  • Simulators – Test and develop robotics virtually
  • Bio-Inspired Swarm Robots – Design intelligent cooperative systems

Led by Team Brainswarm Robotics, this hands-on workshop is designed to empower young minds with practical skills and insights that go far beyond programming. Dive into real-world robotics and experience the fusion of innovation and automation.
